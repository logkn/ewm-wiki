# Monroe Hammerfist

Monroe Hammerfist is a senior dwarven craftsman (roughly 250 years old) and long-time head of the [Tri-Hilt Guild](../Organizations/tri-hilt-guild.mdx). He is known for piercing blue eyes, a salt-and-pepper beard (heavy on the salt), and a booming laugh he shares with those he trusts.
